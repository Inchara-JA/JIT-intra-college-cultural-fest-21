WAR OF WORDS

WAR OF WORDS

RULES:
* This is a solo event.
* Event takes place in 3 rounds.
* Topic for the first round will be given 10 minutes prior to the event individually.
* The participants will be given a time limit of 2+1 minutes for each round.
* In the first round the participants are required to introduce themselves and state their arguments regarding the topic.
* Based on their performance, participants will be selected for second round.
* Topic for second round will be given 10 minutes prior to the round.
* Topic and Opponents will be chosen by the judge.
* Based on toss, participants will be allowed to speak for or against the topic.
* According to their performance, participants will be selected for third round.
* The third round will be a rebuttal round. Every participant can rebut their competitors for 1 minute.
* In the third round the participants can choose to rebut their opponents or present their arguments and present a conclusion for their argument.
